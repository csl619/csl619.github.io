Who am I?

I am a self taught Python & Web developer with a background in Accounting and Finance.

My career started out in a finance role that soon expanded into business process efficiency due to a desire to reduce the repetative nature of my weekly tasks. Looking at the options to automate these processes I stumbled upon Python. As I expanded my knowledge base I began looking at web applications to further assist these processes and have spent the last 2 years creating custom applications for a number of businesses using Django.
